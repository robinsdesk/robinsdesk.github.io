OUR STORY

On the Road to Pizza Perfection

Urbanslice started as a venture between two long-time pizza and travel nerds: Margo, an urban planner and landscaper and Rita, who carries a decade of baking experience in a traditional pizzeria. Since its debut in spring 2011, their flagship branches in Old City and South Philadelphia has expanded to vibrant districts in Chicago and San Francisco. Our flour, cheeses, meats, and produce are locally sourced to get it as close as we can to the unique taste of those regions. We’ll go to just about any length and expense to get our ingredients locally. Likewise, we have taken care to craft every part of the UrbanSlice experience – from the décor to the service – to embody our approach. Make sure to keep your eyes peeled for our year-round deals and events on Twitter and Facebook!

 

Now Available: Pocket Guide to Ten Regional Pizzas

More than a century ago, pizza began as humble street food among Italian-Americans in lower Manhattan. Since then, folks from across the country have taken pizza and made it their own. From the cooking method to the ingredients, each pie reflects the local culture it hails from. As long as the people are willing to experiment, the remarkable story of pizza will continue. This mini pocket guide will give you a taste of the culture and history behind the dishes on our menu. At UrbanSlice, we hope that our diverse line of pizzas will find a home in the hearts and bellies of our customers.
